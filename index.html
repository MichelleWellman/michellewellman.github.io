About Me

My name is Michelle Wellman and I am a recent graduate from Mount Holyoke College with a bachelor's degree in physics and astronomy. My ultimate goal is to attend graduate school to get a PhD in physics and become a physics professor in the future. In my spare time I like to play ultimate frisbee, practice various forms of art, and cook.

On this website you will find blog posts on things that interest me such as physics and science, details on my expereinces as a physics student, as well as some of my original work.
